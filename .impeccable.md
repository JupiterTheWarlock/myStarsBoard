## Design Context

### Users
Developers who use GitHub stars as a bookmarking system. They browse their starred repos to find tools, libraries, and inspiration. Usage context: quick lookup during coding sessions, casual browsing on breaks.

### Brand Personality
Warm, terminal-authentic, refined. Three words: **warm, terminal, curated**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Elegant terminal UI — not raw/brutalist, but a luxury interpretation of the terminal aesthetic. Inspired by beautifully customized terminal setups (iTerm2/Warp with精心 configured themes). Claude's signature orange-amber on deep warm black. Monospace typography throughout with Courier Prime as the voice. ASCII box-drawing characters and tree connectors (├── └──) for structure. Table-of-contents dotted leaders for tag headers.

**Theme**: Dark (terminal context — developers in coding sessions).

**Anti-references**: Generic dark dashboards, glassmorphism, rounded cards with gradients, "AI slop" aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. **Terminal as luxury**: Every detail should feel like a beautifully configured terminal, not a raw command line
2. **Text-only texture**: No images, no avatars — structure comes from monospace alignment and ASCII characters
3. **Restrained glow**: Claude's amber used sparingly as accent — overuse kills its power
4. **ASCII structure**: Box-drawing characters and tree connectors provide visual hierarchy without borders or cards
5. **Warm black**: Every surface is warm-tinted — no pure black or cool grays
